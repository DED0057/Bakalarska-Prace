Title: 3D Plugin for WordPress - Shows easily your 3D-Models with WebGL in the format: .stl .obj .dae

== Description ==

With our free plugin Canvasio3D Light can you show your 3D Models in your WordPress website.

Upload your 3D-Models easily with the WordPress Media Manager on your website, in the 3d file format: .obj & .mtl, .stl and .dae for 3D printing, engineering, or simple your designs as interactive 3D-Models.

Insert a Short-Code in a single side with one click on the 3D-Icon above to the WordPress Text editor. Canvasio3D Light use the 3D-Engine "three.js" for WebGL.

If you looking for a "build in" 3D-Viewer for Woocommerce to show your products as 3D-Models, visit our page at [Canvasio3D Woo](https://www.canvasio3d.com/canvasio3d-woo/)

== Installation ==

Installing the plugin takes less than one minute. 

1. Download "canvasio3d-light.zip" and install the plugin

2. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress

3. Use the "3D"-Icon in your WordPress Text editor to insert a Short-Code for your 3D-Model
